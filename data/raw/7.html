Girl sleeping (Sketch -- 11th grade Spanish class)

Title text: I don't remember her name at all, but she fell asleep on the floor in front of me.

Explanation[edit]

This comic does not present a particular point; it is just a picture drawn by Randall. It is just what the title says - a sketch of a girl sleeping drawn during a Spanish class.

According to the title text, she is also on the floor.

Transcript[edit]

[Girl sleeping on her side, facing away from view.]

Trivia[edit]

  • This was the 1st comic originally posted to LiveJournal.
    • The next was 4: Landscape (sketch).
  • Original title: "Girl sleeping"
  • Original Randall quote: "I drew this in 11th-grade Spanish class. We were watching a movie and she was asleep on the floor in front of me."
  • This was one of the thirteen first comics posted to LiveJournal within 12 minutes on Friday September 30, 2005.
  • This comic was posted on xkcd when the web site opened on Sunday the 1st of January 2006.
    • It was posted along with all 41 comics posted before that on LiveJournal as well as a few others.
    • The latter explaining why the numbers of these 41 LiveJournal comics ranges from 1-44.
  • One of the original drawings drawn on checkered paper.
  • The drawing returns in 1506: xkcloud as one of the Help! We lost the text given images.
